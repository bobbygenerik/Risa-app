## 2026-03-14 - Added Tooltips to IconButtons
**Learning:** For accessibility in Flutter, icon-only `IconButton` widgets must include a descriptive `tooltip` property. This ensures screen readers can announce the button's action and helps users who rely on hover tooltips to understand icon-only actions.
**Action:** When adding or reviewing `IconButton` widgets that lack text labels, always ensure a `tooltip` string is provided.
